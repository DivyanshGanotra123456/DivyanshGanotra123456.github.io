Community Guidelines:

Be respectful: Treat others with respect and kindness. Do not use hate speech, make personal attacks, or engage in any behavior that may be seen as abusive or harassing.

Be honest: Provide honest and constructive feedback about books. Do not misrepresent yourself or provide false information.

Follow community standards:Follow all applicable laws, regulations, and community standards when using the platform.

Stay on topic: Keep your reviews and comments focused on the book and its content. Do not use the platform to promote unrelated products or services.

Report violations: If you encounter any violations of these guidelines or any inappropriate behavior, report it to the platform's administrators.

Be mindful of others: Do not post spoilers or reveal critical plot points without warning. Be considerate of others who may not have finished reading the book.

Respect copyrights: Do not plagiarize or violate the intellectual property rights of others. Only post original content or content that you have permission to share.







Frequently Asked Questions:

How do I rate a book?

To rate a book, search for it in the app and click on its cover image to open its details page. There, you will see an option to rate the book on a scale of 1 to 5 stars. Simply click on the appropriate star to submit your rating.

How can I see the books I've rated in the past?

To view the books you've rated, go to your profile page and click on the "My Ratings" tab. This will display a list of all the books you've rated along with their respective ratings.

Can I change the rating I've given to a book?

Yes, you can change your rating for a book at any time. Simply go to the book's details page and click on the stars to select a new rating. Your previous rating will be replaced with the new one.

How are book ratings calculated?

Book ratings are calculated based on the average of all the ratings given to the book by users of the app. For example, if three users have given a book ratings of 3, 4, and 5 stars respectively, the book's overall rating would be (3+4+5)/3 = 4 stars.

Can I rate a book without writing a review?

Yes, you can rate a book without writing a review. Simply select your rating using the star system on the book's details page and click "Submit".

Can I see reviews other users have written for a book?

Yes, you can see reviews written by other users for a book. Simply go to the book's details page and scroll down to the "Reviews" section. There, you will see a list of all the reviews that have been written for the book.

is it possible to see the most popular books on the app?

Yes, you can see the most popular books on the app by selecting the "Popular" tab on the app's homepage. This will display a list of the books that have received the most ratings and reviews from users of the app.
